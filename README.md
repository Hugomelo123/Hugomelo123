## The Problem
Information is not missing.  
It is misplaced.

I work between **field and office** to make operations visible, structured, and usable.

---

## Pattern I see on sites
- Calls replace records  
- WhatsApp replaces structure  
- Office has no real visibility  
- Problems appear too late  

Not a people problem.  
An information flow problem.

---

## Impact
- Delays discovered late  
- Costs invisible until the end  
- Managers react instead of act  
- Decisions made on partial data  

---

## What I do
I take real situations and build **operational demos** to show:

→ where information breaks  
→ what changes if the flow is structured  
→ how to keep it simple (no ERP, no extra work)

Not products.  
Working thinking.
